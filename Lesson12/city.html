About Lerma de Villada

The pre-Hispanic name of Lerma, in the Mexican language is Cacamilhuacan, a word in Nahuatl that is composed of cacalotl: "Cuervo"; Milli: "sementera"; Hua: possessional and can: "where", which together means: "Where there are crows in the cement plants".

The Church of Santa Clara, located in the municipal seat, built at the end of the 16th century and completed in 1613. In 1535 Vasco de Quiroga bought the lands of Tultepec from the chief of Ocoyoacac. It is possible that from the Encomienda to Isabel Moctezuma the mayor was also appointed to the town of Tlalaxco, since in 1613, when Martín Reolín Marejón requested the founding of a new city or town, Alonso de la Loa was found as corregidor .

The Municipality of Lerma is one of the 125 municipalities in which the State of Mexico is divided, a member of the Metropolitan Zone of Toluca, its municipal seat is the city of Lerma de Villada.

Las Plazas Outlet Lerma opened its doors to the public 19 years ago, being the first “Outlet mall” in the Mexican Republic. This departmental complex receives hundreds of thousands of visitors every year who travel from different places to walk its corridors in search of the most prestigious national and foreign brands such as Palacio de Hierro, H&M, Nike Factory Store, Coach, Adolfo Domínguez, Guess, American Eagle, Hugo Boss, Lacoste, Cloe, Brooks Brothers, Tommy Hilfiger, Under Armor, Calvin Klein, Osh Kosh, Náutica, Salvatore Ferragamo, among others.

Of the ancient settlers settled in the original territory, we find the Otomíana family of which we only consider three: Otomí, Mazahuas, and Matlatzincas.
